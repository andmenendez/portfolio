Uniqueness & Competitiveness

Competing against CMS platforms like Wix, Squarespace & Wordpress is arguably impossible. They supply excellent tools for even the most naive of laymen to create their own websites. These services are also inexpensive & quickly deployable as only software stands between the client & his website.

Yet in this flood of CMS, DIY & website builder platforms comes a toll of creativity. Most blogs, journals, & portfolios all conform to a sort of standard limited by these platforms. For this reason wordpress has overtaken a large part of the market as users can code a substantial amount of their own website if desired. Yet even at this point there is an arguable lack of creativity.

Common websites I run into are static & boring. Nobody remembers them. Yet when we run into the websites of renown designers & developers we become captured. I find myself immersed & entertained as I navigate through the webpages of these artists. Some excellent examples that inspire me are:

GMunk, Alex Coven, Martin Ehrlich
